About

Empowering communities through financial excellence and sustainable development

Our History

Established in 1963, Landbank has been at the forefront of rural development and financial inclusion in the Philippines.

Our Growth

From a small agricultural bank to one of the country's largest financial institutions, serving millions of Filipinos.

Our Impact

Supporting farmers, fisherfolk, and rural communities through innovative financial solutions and sustainable programs.

Our Mission

To promote countryside development through accessible and innovative financial solutions, while maintaining financial sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the leading financial institution in countryside development, fostering inclusive growth and sustainable progress.
